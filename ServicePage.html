Facial peeling/scrubs we have

Ferment enzyme peeling:

A purely professional product, peels of the dead skin without rubbing. Ideal for irritated skins. A Physiological Peeling based on natural enzymes.The process: Cover hairline and eyes.
We cleanse the skin and tone it. Mix 1-2 spoons of the ferment enzyme peeling with lukewarm water, into a thick pasted. Apply to face, neck and decollete using a flat brush.
Then keep the peeling wet and constantly warm on the skin for 15-20 minutes. At last we remove it thoroughly with a moist compress or face sponge
with plenty of water.

Fruit enzyme peeling serum neutralizer:

Purely professional product. The Process: Cover hairline and eyes. We cleanse the skin and tone it. We apply the fruit gel swiftly on the face neck and decollete using a flat brush.
Leave it to work 3-4 mintues. Use cooling face sponge /cold water to remove the fruit gel. To finish the peeling we apply
the serum neutraliser generously.Allow to take effect 3-4 minutes. Remove thoughly with a moist compress or face sponge with plenty of water. (Fruit enzyme peeling should not be used
on damaged / irritated skin).

Mild facial Exfoliator/Scrub:

A peeling for mild exfoliation and gental removal of dead skin.The Process: Cover hairline and eyes. We Cleanse the skin and tone it. Apply the facial exfoliator
generously, rub well and gently with moist finger tips. Remove with plenty of water using a compress or face sponge.
