About The Author

Regina Ott-Magoba

Hi Im Regina, I was born in January, 27, 1960. I'm currently 57 and managing Rena Parlor. I first Had the idea on Rena Parlor, in 2010 .
I was at home in Entebbe for a 6 months holiday. It was a sabattical hoilday, of duty. I was in need of a facial treatment, so i went looking for a beauty parlor in Entebbe town.
I finally found one and had a treatment. After the treatment I got the idea of opening a beauty parlour. Finally Rena Parlor was founded 7 years later in 2017.
The name rena beauty parlor was originaly supposed to be Rena Beauty Lounge, because of all the many mushrooming parlors.
